Airdrop Hunter's Toolkit

Essential tools and resources for finding and qualifying for crypto airdrops.

Web3 Wallet (MetaMask / Rabby)

The foundation of airdrop hunting. Rabby Wallet is recommended for its superior security features and transaction simulation.

Revoke.cash

Essential for security. Regularly check and revoke allowances for smart contracts you no longer use to protect your funds.

Visit Revoke.cash

Tracking Spreadsheet

Organization is key. Keep track of every protocol you interact with, the date, volume, and transaction count. This helps you monitor your eligibility.

Social Media (Twitter & Discord)

Most projects announce airdrops and criteria on Twitter and Discord. Create a dedicated "burner" social profile for airdrop tasks.

Gas Tracker

Monitor gas fees to interact with contracts when the network is cheap. Etherscan Gas Tracker is a reliable source.

VPN

Some airdrops are geo-restricted. A reliable VPN can help you access protocols that might be blocked in your region.
