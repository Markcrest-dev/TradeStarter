Forex Trading Checklist

Review this checklist before entering any trade to ensure discipline and consistency.

1. Market Condition Analysis

Is the market trending or ranging? Have I identified the key support and resistance levels? What is the dominant trend on the higher timeframe?

2. Economic Calendar Check

Are there any high-impact news events (Red Folders) scheduled soon? Avoid trading right before major announcements like NFP or FOMC.

3. Entry Signal Confirmation

Does the setup meet all the rules of my trading strategy? Am I entering at a logical level (e.g., pullback to support)?

4. Risk Management

Have I calculated my position size correctly? Is my risk per trade within my limit (e.g., 1-2%)? Is my Stop Loss placed at a technical invalidation point?

5. Risk-to-Reward Ratio

Is the potential reward at least 1.5x or 2x the risk? Does the target profit level look realistic based on recent price action?

6. Mental State

Am I calm and focused? Am I trading out of boredom, revenge, or FOMO? If I'm emotional, I should step away.
