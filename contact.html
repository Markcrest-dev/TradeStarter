Frequently Asked Questions

Is TradeStarter free to use?

Yes! All our educational content, tools, and resources are completely free. We believe in making crypto and forex education accessible to everyone.

Do you provide financial advice?

No, we provide educational content only. All information on TradeStarter is for educational purposes and should not be considered financial advice. Always do your own research.

How do I get started with crypto?

Start with our Crypto Basics page to learn the fundamentals. Then set up a wallet and start with small amounts to practice.

Are the airdrop opportunities legitimate?

We list popular airdrop opportunities, but always encourage you to do your own research. Never share private keys or send crypto to participate in airdrops.

Can I suggest new content or features?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our community. Use the contact form above or reach out on social media with your suggestions.

Do you offer one-on-one coaching?

Currently, we focus on providing free educational content. For personalized guidance, we recommend joining our community where experienced members share insights.
